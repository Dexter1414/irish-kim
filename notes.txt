INSTALLING GIT-SCM & GITHUB PRE-DEPLOYMENT

INSTALLATION FOR GIT-SCM

1. Go to, https://git-scm.com/
2. Click for "Install for Windows" then click the "Click here to download".
3. Once it download, click the download file.
4. Once you clicked it, click next.
5. Next again.
6. Next Again.
7. In the choices of, "Let Git decide" & "Override the default branch name for new repositories" 
click the "Override the default branch name for new repositories".
8. Once you next it, Click the "Git from the command line and also from 3rd-party software"
9. Once you next it, pick the choice of "Use bundled OpenSSH"
10. Click, next-next and click install.
